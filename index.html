DeWordle is a "game" based on Wordle. In Wordle, you guess words, and it gives you back colored squares. In DeWordle, you "guess" colored squares, and it gives you back words - all the words that might have been Wordle answers in games that could make the same guess patterns! (Read more here.) Rules for what the colors mean are the same (🟩🟧: correct letter in correct position, 🟨🟦: correct letter in wrong position, ⬜⬛: incorrect letter). See how quickly you can guide it to only one word remaining! (Tap the boxes with dotted-line borders to change their colors.)
